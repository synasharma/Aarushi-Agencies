About us

AAPL is one of leading and trusted solution providers for over 25 years in the printing industry, based at Chandigarh .We are serving more than 700 clients with a turnover of US$ 500 million in 2016-2021. We have an excellent track record of trading of printing inks, printing plates, press room chemicals, printing blankets and other allied products required for the printing and packaging industry. We represent some of the leading companies manufacturing high quality printing consumables in the Indian market,. We aim to provide our customers the best quality products at the right price with excellent service.
